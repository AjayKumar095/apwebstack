Privacy & Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

Welcome to APWebStack. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our services. This document outlines our terms of service and our privacy policy. Please read it carefully before using our services.

Terms of Service

Scope of Work We only develop and code websites. Our expertise is focused on providing high-quality web development services from design to deployment.
Exclusions & Add-ons Hosting, domains, databases, and third-party APIs are not included in development cost. These must be purchased separately or arranged with additional charges.
Revisions & Updates We offer up to 10 free small updates and up to 2 major updates after delivery. Additional changes may involve extra cost.
Additional Integrations Any integration requiring third-party services will be the responsibility of the client.
Payment Terms A 25% non-refundable advance is required to begin work. Remaining payment is required at delivery.
Design Process If no design is provided, we offer 3 different design styles for you to choose from before development begins.
Project Deadlines We ensure on-time delivery. If we fail to meet the agreed deadline, we offer a 10% discount.
Acceptable Use Policy

We only build and host legitimate websites. We do not work on illegal content, gambling, adult websites, or unethical projects. We reserve the right to refuse service.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Data Collection

We collect information entered by you, including name, email, phone, and project details.

Data Usage

We use your information to respond to inquiries and deliver services. We never sell your data.

Cookie Policy

We use cookies to analyze website traffic and enhance your user experience.

Data Security

We take strong precautions to secure your data, though no online system can be 100% secure.

Third-Party Links

We may link to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be reflected with a new “Last Updated” date.
